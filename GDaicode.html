Geometry Dash

The Rhythm-Based Platformer That Tests Human Limits

What is Geometry Dash?

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer created by RobTop Games. Players control a geometric icon and must jump, fly, flip gravity, and time their movements perfectly to avoid obstacles.

Every level is synced to music, meaning success depends on timing, memory, and extreme precision.

Geometry Dash is known for its brutal difficulty, massive community, and user-created levels that push the game far beyond its original design.

Difficulty System

Easy

Very forgiving, designed for beginners.

Normal

Introduces tighter jumps and rhythm.

Hard

Requires practice and memorization.

Harder

Fast-paced with little room for error.

Insane

Extremely precise gameplay.

Demon

The hardest category in the game.

Demon levels are further split into: Easy Demon, Medium Demon, Hard Demon, Insane Demon, and Extreme Demon. Extreme Demons can take thousands of attempts to beat.

History of Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash was released in 2013. Originally, it was a simple mobile game with a few official levels.

Over time, RobTop added a level editor, allowing players to create their own levels. This transformed the game into a massive creative platform.

Today, Geometry Dash has millions of levels and one of the most dedicated gaming communities ever.

Fun Facts

  • Some levels take over 100,000 attempts to verify.
  • Geometry Dash levels can desync if your FPS changes.
  • Many extreme demons are harder than any official level.
  • The game still receives updates years later.
  • Players often use muscle memory more than reaction time.
